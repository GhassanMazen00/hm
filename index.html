Botox (Anti-wrinkle / Face shaping)

Botox treatments are used to temporarily relax facial muscles that cause dynamic wrinkles, such as forehead lines, frown lines, and crow's feet. The treatment can also be used for facial shaping, including jawline contouring and brow lifting.

Treatment Benefits:

  • Reduces appearance of fine lines and wrinkles
  • Prevents formation of new wrinkles
  • Non-surgical brow lift and eye opening
  • Jawline contouring for slimmer appearance
  • Treatment for excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis)
  • Quick procedure with minimal downtime

Treatment Duration: 15-30 minutes | Downtime: None | Results Appear: 3-7 days | Duration: 3-6 months

Fillers (Face contour & enhancement)

Dermal fillers are gel-like substances injected beneath the skin to restore lost volume, smooth lines, enhance facial contours, and rejuvenate the overall appearance. Different types of fillers are used for various areas and concerns.

Common Treatment Areas:

  • Cheek augmentation and volume restoration
  • Nasolabial fold (smile line) correction
  • Lip enhancement and definition
  • Jawline and chin contouring
  • Under-eye tear trough rejuvenation
  • Marionette lines and oral commissures

Treatment Duration: 30-60 minutes | Downtime: Mild swelling/bruising (1-3 days) | Results: Immediate | Duration: 6-24 months depending on product

Skin Boosters / Glow Injections

Skin boosters are injectable treatments containing hyaluronic acid that hydrate the skin from within, improving texture, elasticity, and radiance. They work by attracting and retaining moisture in the skin for a lasting healthy glow.

Treatment Benefits:

  • Deep skin hydration and moisturization
  • Improved skin texture and elasticity
  • Reduced appearance of fine lines
  • Enhanced skin radiance and glow
  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Suitable for all skin types and ages

Treatment Duration: 30-45 minutes | Downtime: Minimal (mild redness) | Recommended: Series of 3 treatments, 4 weeks apart | Duration: 6-9 months

PRP / Plasma (Glow + Hair)

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy uses your own blood's growth factors to stimulate healing and regeneration. For skin, it rejuvenates and improves texture; for hair, it stimulates follicles and promotes growth.

Treatment Applications:

  • Skin rejuvenation and texture improvement
  • Reduction of fine lines and wrinkles
  • Treatment of hair loss (androgenetic alopecia)
  • Improvement of skin tone and elasticity
  • Scar revision and wound healing
  • Natural approach using patient's own blood

Treatment Duration: 60-90 minutes | Downtime: Mild redness (1-2 days) | Recommended: Series of 3-4 treatments | Results: Gradual improvement over months

Laser Hair Removal

Laser hair removal uses concentrated light beams to target and destroy hair follicles, preventing or delaying future hair growth. It's effective for most areas of the body and suitable for various skin types with advanced technology.

Treatment Areas:

  • Face: upper lip, chin, sideburns
  • Underarms and bikini area
  • Legs: full legs or partial
  • Back and chest
  • Arms and shoulders
  • Precision areas: eyebrows, ears

Treatment Duration: 15-60 minutes (depending on area) | Downtime: Minimal | Recommended: 6-8 sessions, 4-6 weeks apart | Results: Permanent reduction, maintenance may be needed

Lasers for Skin Tone & Pigmentation

Advanced laser technology targets melanin in the skin to treat various pigmentation concerns including sun spots, age spots, melasma, freckles, and uneven skin tone. Different wavelengths are used for specific pigment types and depths.

Conditions Treated:

  • Sun damage and age spots (lentigines)
  • Melasma and hormonal pigmentation
  • Freckles and ephelides
  • Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation
  • Uneven skin tone and texture
  • Certain types of birthmarks

Treatment Duration: 20-45 minutes | Downtime: 3-7 days (crusting/darkening) | Recommended: 3-5 sessions | Results: Significant improvement after complete healing

Acne Scars + Texture Improvement

Combination treatments to address various types of acne scars (ice pick, rolling, boxcar) and improve overall skin texture through collagen remodeling. Treatments are customized based on scar type, depth, and skin condition.

Treatment Approaches:

  • Fractional laser resurfacing
  • Microneedling with PRP
  • Chemical peels (medical grade)
  • Subcision for rolling scars
  • TCA cross for ice pick scars
  • Dermal fillers for atrophic scars

Treatment Duration: 30-90 minutes | Downtime: 3-10 days (depending on treatment) | Recommended: Series of 3-6 treatments | Results: Progressive improvement over months

Other Aesthetic Laser Services

Comprehensive laser treatments for various aesthetic concerns including vascular lesions, skin tightening, tattoo removal, and overall skin rejuvenation. Advanced laser technology allows for targeted treatment of specific concerns with minimal downtime.

Additional Laser Services:

  • Vascular laser for spider veins and redness
  • Skin tightening and collagen stimulation
  • Tattoo removal (various colors)
  • Non-ablative skin rejuvenation
  • Treatment of warts and skin lesions
  • Laser genesis for overall skin quality

Treatment Duration: 15-60 minutes | Downtime: Minimal to moderate | Recommended: Varies by condition | Results: Treatment-specific outcomes
